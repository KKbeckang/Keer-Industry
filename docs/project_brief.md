## 2. Site Goals

1. **Brand Authority:** Establish Keer Industry as a professional and reliable supplier in the industrial sector.
2. **Lead Generation:** Convert visitors into qualified leads through inquiry forms and direct contact.
3. **Information Accessibility:** Provide engineers with easy access to technical specifications and manuals.

## 3. Success Metrics

1. **Inquiry Rate:** Number of quote requests / Total visitors > 2%.
2. **Engagement:** Average session duration > 2 minutes (indicating technical reading).
3. **Download Activity:** Number of datasheet/manual downloads per month.
